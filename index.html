About us

Florentine

Multi-cuisine

FLORENTINE is a multi-cuisine, fine dining restaurant which aims to bring the best-in class hospitality and dining experience to its customers. It has great ambience with ultra-chic decor and pastel color palettes. It serves meals of different cuisines across the world with some signature dishes offered by its Michelin starred chef, who is also one of the owners of the restaurant. The place also has an attractive bar lounge with unique cocktails on its menu.
